Element Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of your game.

There are four elements below (earth, wind, water, fire). By clicking on an element your will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each element is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
